Understanding the Results

Tau Point: The estimated treatment effect for each drug. A more negative value indicates a stronger potential benefit.
CI High (one-sided 90%): The upper bound of the one-sided 90% confidence interval. If CI High is below zero, the treatment effect is statistically significant in the beneficial direction.
CI Low: Not applicable for one-sided tests — displayed as −∞ where the lower bound is unbounded.
